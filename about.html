ABOUT

Noémi Sarpe (1998) is a fashion and fine art photographer from the Netherlands.
Her work is cinematic, atmospheric, and story driven. Moving between fashion campaigns, editorial photography, and collectible fine art, she creates images with a strong sense of mood, styling, and nostalgia. Her visual world is shaped by cinema, travel, old glamour. The result is imagery that feels refined, intimate, and quietly iconic.

Her fine art collection VOYAGE VOYAGE explores travel and the love for the aesthetic that lived long before she did. This collection has been shown at international art fairs and acquired by private collectors.

My work is kind of like your favorite old movie. Classic, cool, and timeless.
