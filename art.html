THE ART COLLECTION

By Noémi Sarpe

"I'm homesick for a time i've never lived"

Noémi: “I’m homesick for a time I have never lived. That is how VOYAGE VOYAGE was born. Not just as a curation of the places I have visited, but of what I have seen, and what I imagined they could have been.

Created during my travels as a fashion photographer, this collection is like my passport: each photograph a stamp, each frame a little movie scene. Inspired by the mood of the 1950s through the 1990s, the work carries a sense of nostalgia, cinema, travel, and timeless escape, like a still from a holiday you never wanted to end.

The images are made to bring atmosphere into your space and collection: warmth, elegance, softness, and a quiet sense of longing.

For those who love to wander, notice beauty in the details, and believe that magic hides in plain sight. If this resonates with you, feel welcome to indulge yourself in my world
